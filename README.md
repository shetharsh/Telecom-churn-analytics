# Telecom Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard 📊

## Key Business Insights

### Insight 1: The "Naked Fiber" Problem
* **Finding:** Fiber optic customers without tech support churn at ~49.4%, compared to just 22.6% if they have the support add-on.
* **Why it matters:** This indicates that the core product (fiber) might have reliability issues or a steep learning curve that frustrates unsupported users.
* **Recommendation:** Bundle a free 3-month tech support trial into all new Fiber Optic sign-ups.

### Insight 2: The High-Risk Newbie Cohort
* **Finding:** Customers in their first year with 2 or fewer services (High Risk Segment) have a 46.8% churn rate, risking ~$73,000 in monthly revenue.
* **Why it matters:** Customers aren't getting entrenched in the ecosystem fast enough.
* **Recommendation:** Implement an aggressive 90-day onboarding email sequence offering heavy discounts on a 3rd service (like Streaming TV or Device Protection) to lock them into the ecosystem.

### Insight 3: The Medium-Risk Revenue Trap
* **Finding:** While the "Medium Risk" segment (1-2 years tenure, 3-4 services) has a lower churn rate (37.3%) than the high-risk group, they represent the largest single pool of monthly revenue ($91,014).
* **Why it matters:** The business is bleeding its most valuable established customers.
* **Recommendation:** Proactively call all Medium Risk customers 30 days before their 1-year or 2-year anniversary with a loyalty upgrade (e.g., free speed boost if they sign an annual contract).
